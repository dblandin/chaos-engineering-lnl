## What are the customer, business, and technical benefits of Chaos Engineering?

</div>

</Layout>

---

<Layout>
  <div style={{ padding: "50px" }}>
    Customer: the increased availability and durability of service means no
    outages disrupt their day-to-day lives.
  </div>
</Layout>

---

<Layout>
  <div style={{ padding: "50px" }}>
    Business: Chaos Engineering can help...
    <div style={{ marginTop: "40px" }}>
      <ul>
        <li>
          help prevent extremely large losses in revenue and maintenance costs
        </li>
        <li>create happier and more engaged engineers</li>
        <li>improve in on-call training for engineering teams</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</Layout>

---

<Layout>
  <div style={{ padding: "50px" }}>
    Technical: the insights from chaos experiments can mean a...
    <div style={{ marginTop: "40px" }}>
    <ul>
      <li>reduction in incidents</li>
      <li>reduction in on-call burden</li>
      <li>increased understanding of system failure modes</li>
      <li>improved system design</li>
      <li>faster mean time to detection for incidents</li>
      <li>reduction in repeated SEVs</li>
    </ul>
      </div>

  </div>
</Layout>
